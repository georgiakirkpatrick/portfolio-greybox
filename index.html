Bio

Picture of Georgia

I grew up in the fair city of Portland, Oregon, where I also earned a biology degree and rejoiced in the nerdy intellectualism of Reed College. Upon graduating, I launched a clothing business inspired by my grandmother's fabric business and organic cotton farmers in Peru, where I spend a fair amount of time and have the good fortune of holding citizenship. I credit entrepreneurship with my cultivation of digital marketing skills and my discovery of programming. Enticed by the power of web development, I enrolled in a coding bootcamp and reveled in a newfound form of creativity. When I'm not coding, I manage social media profiles for mission-driven businesses, blog about women's clothing with pockets, and plot my next trip to the rainforest.
